Working @ Z

What does it take to become “a Zalando”? Above all, it requires passion: to experiment, learn, fail, and repeat the process, so that we get stronger and better every day. Zalando Tech includes men and women from more than 50 different nations and representing a seemingly endless number of interests, hobbies, programming-language preferences, personality types and other characteristics. What unites us is the energy and enthusiasm we share in tackling our common purpose: to deliver award-winning, best-in-class shopping experiences to our +15 million customers.

Radical Agility

Building the world’s most innovative online fashion platform isn’t where our common purpose ends, however. Zalandos are driven to build a tech culture that reinforces Purpose, Autonomy and Mastery. With these three principles to guide us, we can ensure that each Zalando continues to grow, learn and thrive.

Purpose

Every Zalando technologist needs to know the WHY behind what they do; answering the WHY is how we achieve purpose. We have created a framework to help you find your purpose with us, to be your best, and to achieve great things.

Autonomy

Zalando promotes autonomy for both individuals and teams. As individuals, we pick our teams, create our own personal growth and development paths, and give each person a voice. At the team level, we self-organize. It’s how we foster trust and respect.

Mastery

Our passion ultimately focuses on investing in our people. We strive to give Zalandos the best support we can: to help you grow, to provide a place where you can achieve mastery and greatness, and where you are inspired every day to do what you do best--both for Zalando and for yourself.

Methods &
Ethics

Okay, so “autonomy, mastery and purpose” are some pretty lofty ideals. You might be wondering now how Zalandos implement these principles in practice. How do we give meaning to these ideals? Here are some examples:

Tools

We follow methodologies and practices that we know free up technologists to work as efficiently, creatively and meaningfully as possible.

  • Agile Coachings
  • Design Thinking
  • Lean Startup

Environment

We are constantly introducing new ways to help our team stretch and grow in new directions. One important leverage is the creation of a working environment, which is hands-on, but also inspiring and a product of collaboration with our team.

  • InnovationLab
  • Eat, chill and play areas
  • UserLabs & Lab Insights

Tech Constitution

We think it’s important to make sure every Zalando understands his or her rights and responsibilities--as well as the rights and responsibilities of our leadership and the entire technology team. Some of our guiding principles:

  • To trust instead of control
  • To build a personal brand related to work
  • To make informed decisions

Community

Zalandos are proud of the strong community we’ve built. Our self-organized guilds, tech talks, training initiatives, and other efforts give us opportunities to build community around the technologies and topics we love. Want to share your knowledge with others? We’ll help you make it happen.

Events

Our events give us opportunities to share our insights and expertise with each other and with the communities at large to which we belong.

  • Hack week
  • Meetups
  • Game Night

Guilds

We’ve self-organized +70 internal guilds to trade knowledge on our myriad interests: Scala, Clojure, open source, parenting, music, tea, gaming etc. If we don’t see a guild we want, we just make one!

  • Technical Guilds
  • Fun Guilds
  • Team & Role Guilds

Knowledge Sharing

We know that every new technology and methodology we implement involves a learning curve. We’re dedicated to helping everyone on our team get up to speed on using the tools they need to work successfully.

  • Tech Talks
  • Product Demos
  • Coder Dojos

Personal Branding

We support your efforts to build your reputation as a tech leader--not only among your Zalando peers, but in the tech industry at large. That’s why we have people on the team to help you.

  • Mentoring
  • Coachings
  • Trainings

Multi Culti Family

Zalando technologists come from more than 50 countries around the world. With so many languages, backgrounds, beliefs and ideas, how do we make it work? By celebrating our diversity and learning from each other. We are all different, but we are all Zalandos.

Our Perks

Zalando’s comprehensive array of benefits emphasize value and fun. Here are some of the perks you’ll get when you join the team:

Food & Drinks

We want every Zalando to be as comfortable as possible during the work day. That’s why we supply lots of beverages, fresh fruit and cereals for everyone’s enjoyment. For the truly hungry, our brand-new food court serves great and healthy food in a relaxed atmosphere.

40% Shopping Discount

Our 40% shopping voucher is one of our greatest benefits. Grow your wardrobe while you grow on the job! And the best thing about shopping at Zalando is that you’re actively testing our shop and providing feedback for improvement.

Lots of fun

Zalandos love to have fun together. Parties, ping pong, video games, shooting pool, movie nights, jamming out in our band rehearsal room, launching shoes into space--we come up with lots of activities to take time out, relax and get to know each other.
