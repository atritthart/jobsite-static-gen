Helsinki is the thriving capital city of Finland, located just eight hours away by plane from Beijing in one direction and New York in the other. It is fast becoming one of the leading tech startup hubs in Europe thanks to its openness to innovation, first-rate universities, well-educated workforce, and high concentration of venture capital. Its atmosphere of entrepreneurship has inspired Zalando and other leading tech companies to establish research and development centers there in recent years.

Helsinki offers a very high standard of living and many amenities. The city combines natural beauty and cosmopolitanism, and everything is within walking distance. You can choose to live in the fast lane or opt for a slower pace of life.
